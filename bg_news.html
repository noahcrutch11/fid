Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. Insous vide cooking you submerge the food (usally vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temprature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it asu;tra-low-temperature poaching..
